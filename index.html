B3_H4RSH3R

A (harsh) noise-based audio game

Tap the pad and move around to generate and shape the sound.

Move the slider to zoom and increment the abrasivness of noise.

Take care of your energy:

high values of focus reduce your energy,

low values of focus allow you to recover energy.

Buttons P1-P8 recall different presets.
